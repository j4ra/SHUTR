SHUTR Game

SHUTR is a third-person survival RPG set in a fantastical low-poly universe. In this game, you embark on an epic journey as a magic apprentice armed with the SHUTR device, capable of traversing the hyperspace.

Video trailer

Your mission is to save the fabric of realities by purging a mysterious entity from higher dimensions. Prepare yourself for a challenging adventure filled with crafting, exploration, and magical combat.
